DINING

A Culinary Journey of Exquisite Dining and
Vibrant Bar Delights

Our signature restaurant, Flava, offers a diverse menu of international and Filipino cuisines. Sip on handcrafted cocktails and admire the carefully curated decor at Flava Lounge & Bar, or grab a quick bite at Flava Lite Rooftop Bar & Cafe.

Flava Restaurant

Enjoy the best that Filipino cuisines have to offer at small prices at our restaurant which is open from 6:30 am to 11:00 pm every day.The seating arrangement is well-spaced, creating an intimate and cosy dining experience that is perfect for both couples and groups.

The casual indoor dining area at our restaurant in Cebu features floor-to-ceiling windows, which provide an exceptional view of Cebu’s bustling cityscape.

Operating Hours: 06:30 AM to 11:00 PM
